About Parnethius

Parnethius is an immortal being, dating back to 800 BXE. He was an extremely powerful figure, living as the most powerful God there was known to Xrete and the whole world. With the power of electrokenesis, nobody could defeat Parnethius. He was a kind man, one that would risk his own life to protect local townsman. In the spring of 712 BXE, he was coronated as King of the Land. However, during his reign, a bitter, scrawny little magician did not approve. She believed that anyone who serves the Kingdom should fall within the sorcers family, and so she spent 100 years concocting an extremely powerful spell to cast him away. On a chilly falls night, the evil witch snuck up Mount Yoyo and cast the spell. And so Parnethius vanished from the city of Xrete and was teleported to an abandoned building which he could not escape. He also turned into a fluffy bear and has been trapped for 2520 years.
