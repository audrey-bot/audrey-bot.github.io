About Luanne

Luanne is a lonely girl, often finding herself alone in her room, wandering aimlessly, or walking through the empty streets kicking cans. Her childhood was marred by a constant sense of uncertainty, as she bounced from place to place, never knowing where she would end up. With no family to turn to and no friends to lean on, she grew up in solitude. As she reached adolescence, she finally found a semblance of companionship with a few individuals in the dark lanes of the city. Despite these connections, her life took a peculiar turn when she encountered the entity known as "the Uglies." There was something eerily familiar about it, an inexplicable pull that she couldn't resist. This enigmatic presence became the focal point of her existence, and she now spends most of her time obsessively fixated on it, trying to unravel its mysteries and understand the profound sense of connection she feels towards it.
